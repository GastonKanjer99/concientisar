Wetland Fires

In the Paraná Delta, recurring burns alter soil structure and transform the ecosystem. The vegetation that regrows is not always the original: grasslands replace forests, and invasive species displace natives. Understanding these changes is key to planning true restoration, not just apparent regeneration.
