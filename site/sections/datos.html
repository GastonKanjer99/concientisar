From signal to insight

  • Land use / boundaries

2. Process

  • Calibration, speckle filtering
  • Co-registration and mosaic
  • Time series and smoothing

3. Metrics

  • Δ backscatter (VV/VH)
  • Moisture / roughness index
  • Recovery time (T90)
